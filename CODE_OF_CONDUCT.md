# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining a respectful, professional, and inclusive environment for everyone participating in the QUASAR project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Welcome different viewpoints.
- Provide helpful feedback.
- Focus discussions on technical improvement.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Offensive language
- Spam or disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation if these guidelines are violated.
