# Research Directions

Although the current implementation is complete, several research extensions may be explored in the future:

- Remaining Useful Life (RUL) prediction for quantum circuits
- Support for additional quantum hardware noise models
- Comparative benchmarking across quantum backends
- Integration with IBM Quantum hardware
- Expanded explainability techniques
- Support for larger OpenQASM programs

These ideas are potential research directions and are not required for the current implementation.
